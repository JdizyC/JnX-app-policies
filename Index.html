What information do we collect?

Privacy Policy & Data Disclosure Effective Date: June 18, 2026 JnX Project Master is designed with a privacy-first, local-only philosophy. We believe your project plans, tasks, and personal notes should remain under your exclusive control. 1. Data Collection and Storage • Local Storage: All application data—including project titles, next steps, update logs, categories, and significance levels—is stored directly on your device using your browser's localStorage (or the equivalent storage on Android). • No Cloud Syncing: This application does not have a central database or cloud backend. Your data is never uploaded to our servers. • User Credentials: If you create an account, your username and a cryptographically hashed version of your password (using bcrypt) are stored locally on your device. We never see or store your plain-text password. 2. Use of Data The data you enter is used exclusively to provide the application's functionality: • Organizing and filtering your projects. • Tracking project history and "Next Steps." • Generating local PDF reports. • Exporting/Importing data via CSV files. 3. Third-Party Services • Google Analytics (Telemetry): We use Google Analytics to understand how the app is used. We track actions (e.g., "created a project", "exported a CSV", "logged in") to help us improve the user experience. • Content Privacy: We do not send the content of your projects (titles, notes, or tasks) to Google Analytics. We only track that an action occurred, and occasionally the count of items involved (e.g., "exported 5 projects"). • AI Services: While the app structure supports AI capabilities, no project data is currently transmitted to external AI providers (such as Google Gemini) unless you explicitly configure and authorize such a connection. 4. Data Security • On-Device Protection: Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on the security of your device and browser. • Password Protection: The built-in "Sign In" feature provides a layer of access control within the browser, but it does not encrypt the underlying storage. • Encryption: We recommend using device-level encryption (like FileVault, BitLocker, or Android's built-in encryption) to further protect your data. 5. Your Rights and Control • Data Portability: You can use the Export CSV feature at any time to download a full copy of your data. • Data Erasure: You can delete individual projects, or clear your entire data set by using the "Clear Site Data" or "Delete Account" functions within your browser/app settings. • Offline Access: The app is designed to function entirely offline, ensuring you have access to your data even without an internet connection. 6. Changes to This Policy We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top will indicate when changes were last made. 7.
